Tables


Tables are good for showing a lot of information. They help to organize the separation, etc. See examples below:

Month Sales
January $ 50,000.00
February $ 10,000.00
March $ 85,000.00
April $ 56,000.00
May $ 98,000.00


Create "Stripped" table adding to<tr> strippedclass

Month Sales
January $ 50,000.00
February $ 10,000.00
March $ 85,000.00
April $ 56,000.00
May $ 98,000.00


Create "Hoverable" table adding to<tr> highlightclass

Month Sales
January $ 50,000.00
February $ 10,000.00
March $ 85,000.00
April $ 56,000.00
May $ 98,000.00 

<!-- NORMAL TABLE -->
<table>
<thead>
<tr>
<th>Month</th>
<th>Sales</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>January</td>
<td>$ 50,000.00</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>February</td>
<td>$ 10,000.00</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>March</td>
<td>$ 85,000.00</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>April</td>
<td>$ 56,000.00</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>May</td>
<td>$ 98,000.00</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
<!-- /NORMAL TABLE -->

<!-- STRIPPED TABLE -->
<table>
<thead>
<tr class="stripped">
<th>Month</th>
<th>Sales</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr class="stripped">
<td>January</td>
<td>$ 50,000.00</td>
</tr>

<td>$ 56,000.00</td>
</tr>
<tr class="stripped">
<td>May</td>
<td>$ 98,000.00</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
<!-- /STRIPPED TABLE -->

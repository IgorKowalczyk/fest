Carousel


Our carousel is a versatile component, you can use it as a slider for photos and other things. You can slide your finger on it, so it's suitable for mobile phones. See examples below:

Slide 1 Title

Slide 2 Title

Slide 2 Description

Slide 3 Title

Slide 3 Description

Slide 4 Title

Slide 4 Description


Note: This is also touch compatible. Try swiping with your finger to scroll through the carousel.


HTML
<!-- CAROUSEL -->
<div id="image-carousel" class="dragdealer active">
<div class="handle">
<div class="slide img1">
<div class="info">
<p class="title">Slide 1 Title</p>
<p class="description"><button class="btn-radius btn-fill ripple ripple-effect">Button</button></p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="slide img2">
<div class="info">
<p class="title">Slide 2 Title</p>
<p class="description">Slide 2 Description</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="slide img3">
<div class="info">
<p class="title">Slide 3 Title</p>
<p class="description">Slide 3 Description</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="slide img4">
<div class="info">
<p class="title">Slide 4 Title</p>
<p class="description">Slide 4 Description</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<!-- /CAROUSEL -->
